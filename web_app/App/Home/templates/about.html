About the Project

Our project is focused on developing an astral classification system that utilizes ensemble learning models to accurately classify and identify astronomical objects based on their spectral characteristics.

The Data Used

The project is built on data from the Sloan Digital Sky Survey (SDSS). The SDSS is a massive astronomical survey that has collected data on millions of celestial objects, making it an ideal dataset for training our classification system. The dataset contains detailed information about the spectral characteristics of various astronomical objects, including their wavelengths and intensities.

Our Aim

With our system we aim to make the classification process easier for all sorts of users, be that technical or non-technical users.
